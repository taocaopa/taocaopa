## 🔬 Research Areas

### 1️⃣ Agentic AI for Healthcare

Designing **multi-agent systems** that coordinate medical knowledge, lifestyle data, and clinical guidelines.

**Core Components:**
- 🩺 Medical Knowledge Agent - Clinical decision support
- 🥗 Nutrition Agent - Personalized dietary guidance
- 🏃 Lifestyle Agent - Activity and sleep optimization
- ⚠️ Risk Prediction Agent - Early warning systems

**Key Innovation:** *Agents collaborate to generate holistic health insights beyond single-domain analysis.*

---

### 2️⃣ Medical Knowledge Systems (RAG)

Transforming medical literature into **queryable intelligence** using retrieval-augmented generation.

**Technical Stack:**
- 📄 Document parsing (PDF, HTML, medical formats)
- 🔍 Semantic search ( embeddings + vector DB)
- 🧠 Context-aware retrieval
- ✅ Fact verification & source attribution

**Impact:** *Make medical knowledge accessible and actionable for AI agents.*

---

### 3️⃣ Personalized Nutrition AI

AI systems that generate **personalized nutrition guidance** based on:

- 🎯 Health goals (weight management, disease prevention)
- 🔬 Biomarker signals (glucose, cholesterol, inflammation)
- 📊 Lifestyle patterns (activity, sleep, stress)
- 📚 Evidence-based medical knowledge

**Approach:** *Combine structured health data with scientific literature for personalized recommendations.*

---

### 4️⃣ Consumer Health AI

Exploring AI systems for **everyday health decision making**.

**Applications:**
- 🤒 Symptom triage assistants
- 💊 Medication adherence copilots
- 🏋️ Lifestyle optimization systems
- 🛡️ Preventative health monitoring

**Paradigm Shift:** *From clinic-centered to AI-powered, personalized, continuous care.*

## 🎯 Vision: The Future of Healthcare

Healthcare today is largely **generalized and reactive**.

Most people receive medical support only when they visit a clinic, yet health outcomes are determined largely by **daily behaviors and long-term lifestyle patterns**.

### The Shift: AI-Powered, Personalized, Continuous

**Instead of interacting with healthcare systems only during illness**, people will have **AI health copilots** that support them every day with:

- 📚 **Knowledge** - Evidence-based health information at their fingertips
- 🛡️ **Prevention** - Early warning systems and risk mitigation
- 💡 **Personalization** - Tailored recommendations based on individual data

### My Contribution

I explore the **technical foundations for building these systems**:

- Architecting multi-agent AI systems
- Designing privacy-first health data platforms
- Creating intuitive natural language interfaces
- Ensuring safety and efficacy in AI health recommendations
